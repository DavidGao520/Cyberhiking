# Cyber Hiking USA - 1-Minute Pitch (English)

## 🎯 Core Pitch (10 seconds)
**Cyber Hiking USA** is a survival simulation game based on the Pacific Crest Trail. Experience the challenge of hiking 2,650 miles from Mexico to Canada, managing resources and making life-or-death decisions in extreme conditions.

## 🎮 Core Gameplay (20 seconds)
- **Realistic Resource Management**: Health, energy, food, water, and mental state - every decision matters
- **Random Event System**: Each step forward triggers real-world scenarios - getting lost, wildlife encounters, severe weather, equipment failures
- **Leave No Trace (LNT) System**: Your choices affect your environmental score, higher scores unlock more positive events
- **Resupply System**: Purchase food and water in towns, plan your resupply strategy

## 💡 Unique Value (20 seconds)
- **Educational Purpose**: Help players understand the power of nature and the fragility of human life through simulation
- **Real Geography**: Based on actual PCT route data and elevation profiles
- **Dynamic Weather**: Elevation affects temperature, weather impacts energy consumption
- **Immersive Experience**: Every click provides feedback, every choice has consequences

## 🚀 Technical Highlights (10 seconds)
- Built with React + Vite
- Data-driven game system
- Responsive design, mobile-friendly
- Real-time state feedback system

## 🎤 Presentation Tips

**Delivery Style:**
- Start with energy and enthusiasm
- Pause after key points (resource management, LNT system)
- Use hand gestures when mentioning "2,650 miles" or "life-or-death"
- Slow down when explaining the educational purpose
- End with a strong call to action

**Key Phrases to Emphasize:**
- "2,650 miles" (pause for impact)
- "Every choice matters"
- "Life-or-death decisions"
- "Understand nature's power"
- "Not about adventure - about understanding"

**Visual Aids (if available):**
- Show game screenshot during "random events" section
- Display LNT score UI when explaining the system
- Show resupply menu when discussing strategy
